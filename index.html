Our Fast-Acting Key Ingredients

Magnesium Silicate

Magnesium Silicate is a naturally occurring clay comprised of magnesium, aluminum, silica, and oxygen. The clay is then purified and refined to a powder that's used to improve the texture and smoothness of aging skin.

Sodium Hyaluronate

Sodium Hyaluronate is an incredible oil-free humectant, capable of attracting and binding 1000X its own weight in water! This essential hydration effectively balances and re-hydrates dry skin without clogging pores.

Retinyl Palmitate

Retinyl Palmitate is a Vitamin A derivative known for its ability to stimulate collagen production, improve cellular proliferation and prevent collagen degradation. Optimizing and supporting healthy collagen levels is key to maintaining skin’s structure, firmness, and youthful suppleness.

Camellia Oleifera Leaf

Camellia Oleifera is a type of green tea plant that's mostly known for the oil that comes from its seeds. It helps to reduce puffy eyes, decrease wrinkles, and increases elasticity of the skin. Camellia Oleifera promotes the health and quantity of collagen while maintaining firm and elastic skin.
